Biography

Interested in engineering solutions to environmental problems. I believe that technological advancements are key to creating a more sustainable planet. I think a holistic approach is necessary to understand the manifold interactions between ecology, green buildings, environmentally friendly transportation modalities and renewable energy generation, in order to cut emissions and create a balance between anthropogenic activities and natural processes. Authorized to work in the EU and USA. See below or my CV & Projects for more.
